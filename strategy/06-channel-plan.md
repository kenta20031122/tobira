# Tobira Channel Plan

## Recommendation

Prioritize two core acquisition channels for the next 90 days:

1. SEO through blog and prefecture guides
2. Instagram through carousels and short visual discovery content

Keep a third lane only as a lightweight repurposing test:

3. Pinterest or other visual-search surfaces, only if Instagram assets can be reused cheaply

## Why these channels fit the current repo

- Blog infrastructure already exists
- Prefecture guides already exist
- Instagram generation and publishing workflows already exist
- The brand is visual and destination-led
- The product has clear landing surfaces for both search and social

## Channel 1: SEO

- Best for:
  - High-intent travelers researching destinations, themes, and itinerary ideas
- Best landing pages:
  - Blog list and article pages for broad search intent
  - Prefecture guides for destination-specific intent
  - Discover page for users who want help narrowing options
- Recommended content pillars:
  - `beyond tokyo` and repeat-visitor destination ideas
  - `without car` travel content
  - seasonal regional content
  - prefecture deep dives
  - sample itineraries for specific travel styles
- Primary CTA:
  - Move readers to `/discover` or `/plan`, not directly to pricing

## Channel 2: Instagram

- Best for:
  - Top-of-funnel inspiration and audience building
- Best landing pages:
  - `/discover` for broad curiosity
  - selected blog or guide pages for topic-specific posts
- Recommended content pillars:
  - hidden regional gems
  - no-car routes
  - seasonal visual hooks
  - interest-based collections such as onsen, history, nature
- Primary CTA:
  - `Find your match` or `Plan your route`, depending on the post intent

## Channel 3: Lightweight visual-search test

- Best for:
  - Evergreen visual discovery if asset reuse is easy
- Best landing pages:
  - Guide pages and evergreen blog posts
- Constraint:
  - Do not build a bespoke workflow yet

## What not to prioritize yet

- Broad paid ads
- Partnerships as a primary growth engine
- Mass-market social posting without a clear funnel destination
- Content that targets generic "Japan travel" with no clear angle

## Recommended content system

- Pillar 1:
  - Beyond Tokyo regional discovery
- Pillar 2:
  - Car-free and practical route planning
- Pillar 3:
  - Seasonal timing and best-time-to-visit planning
- Pillar 4:
  - Prefecture personality and deep-dive guides

## Suggested near-term experiments

1. Compare article CTA to `/discover` versus `/plan`
2. Compare Instagram posts that sell `hidden gems` versus `build your route`
3. Compare broad blog topics versus tighter repeat-visitor topics
