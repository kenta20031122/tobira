# Tobira Deep Strategy Synthesis

This document is the integrated strategy summary for the next 90 days.

Status: Superseded by `08-gtm-integration.md` for the most complete current version.

## Working thesis

Tobira should grow by helping a narrow, high-intent traveler segment go from "I want a more original Japan trip" to "I now have a usable itinerary" faster and with more confidence than blogs, generic AI chat, or mainstream guide content.

## Current direction

- Focus ICP:
  - English-speaking independent travelers planning their second Japan trip, or their first trip beyond the Tokyo-Kyoto-Osaka route
- Main product objective:
  - Increase `planner_completed per qualified visitor`
- Main paid value:
  - Planning confidence, not just premium content access
- Main channels:
  - SEO
  - Instagram
