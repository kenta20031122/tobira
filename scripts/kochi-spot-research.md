## 追加候補 5 件

### 1. Chikurin-ji Temple / 竹林寺
- **Prefecture**: Kochi
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: spiritual
- **Description**: One of Kochi's most famous temples, Chikurin-ji is the 31st stop on the Shikoku 88 Temple Pilgrimage and is widely visited for its mossy grounds, five-storied pagoda, and historic garden. It is a mainstream Kochi classic for travelers who want a major spiritual and cultural landmark without leaving the city area.
- **Address (English)**: 3577 Godaisan, Kochi, Kochi 781-8125, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.5466, 133.5775
- **Admission**: Free for the temple grounds; JPY 800 for adults for the garden, treasure hall, and study hall area
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn, especially for fresh greenery and fall foliage
- **Access**: About 20 minutes by car from Kochi IC, or take the MY-YU Bus from Kochi Station and get off at `Chikurinji Temple`
- **Suggested stay**: 1.5-2 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.chikurinji.com/

### 2. The Kochi Prefectural Makino Botanical Garden / 高知県立牧野植物園
- **Prefecture**: Kochi
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: nature
- **Description**: Kochi's flagship botanical garden is one of the prefecture's best-known attractions, combining panoramic Mt. Godai scenery with extensive seasonal plant displays and a modern greenhouse. Its strong national profile rose further after renewed interest in botanist Tomitaro Makino, making it one of Kochi's most visited cultural-nature stops.
- **Address (English)**: 4200-6 Godaisan, Kochi, Kochi 781-8125, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.5467, 133.5778
- **Admission**: JPY 850 for adults
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring to early summer and autumn are especially rewarding
- **Access**: About 20 minutes by car from central Kochi, or about 30 minutes from Kochi Station by MY-YU Bus
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.makino.or.jp/multilingual/?lang=en

### 3. Noichi Zoological Park of Kochi Prefecture / 高知県立のいち動物公園
- **Prefecture**: Kochi
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: This is one of Kochi's biggest family attractions, known for spacious habitat-style exhibits where animals are shown in more natural environments. It is an established, high-appeal half-day destination for both domestic travelers and families based around Kochi City.
- **Address (English)**: 738 Otani, Noichi-cho, Konan, Kochi 781-5233, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.5711, 133.7058
- **Admission**: JPY 470 for adults; free for high school students and younger
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn for the most comfortable walking weather
- **Access**: About 20 minutes by car from Nankoku IC, or about 20 minutes on foot from Noichi Station on the Gomen-Nahari Line
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://noichizoo.or.jp/en/

### 4. Kami City Yanase Takashi Memorial Hall Anpanman Museum / 香美市立やなせたかし記念館アンパンマンミュージアム
- **Prefecture**: Kochi
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: Dedicated to Kochi-born creator Takashi Yanase, this museum is one of the prefecture's most recognizable family destinations and a pilgrimage stop for Anpanman fans nationwide. It is a firmly established sightseeing draw in eastern Kochi rather than a niche local museum.
- **Address (English)**: 1224-2 Birafu, Kahoku-cho, Kami, Kochi 781-4212, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.6477, 133.7838
- **Admission**: JPY 1,200 for adults for the common ticket covering the main memorial hall facilities
- **Best season**: Year-round
- **Access**: About 35 minutes by car from Nankoku IC, or take the JR Dosan Line to Tosa-Yamada Station and transfer to the JR Bus Odochi Line for about 25 minutes to `Anpanman Museum Mae`
- **Suggested stay**: 1.5-2.5 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://anpanman-museum.net/

### 5. The Sakamoto Ryoma Memorial Museum / 高知県立坂本龍馬記念館
- **Prefecture**: Kochi
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: history
- **Description**: One of Kochi's signature museums, this hilltop memorial museum above Katsurahama presents the life and legacy of Sakamoto Ryoma, one of Japan's most beloved historical figures. It is a standard stop for first-time visitors who want a major Kochi attraction tied to the prefecture's most famous local hero.
- **Address (English)**: 830 Urado-shiroyama, Kochi, Kochi 781-0262, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.4964, 133.5718
- **Admission**: JPY 500 for adults during regular periods; JPY 900 during planned exhibitions
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring and autumn are especially pleasant for combining with the Katsurahama area
- **Access**: About 2 minutes on foot from `Ryoma Kinenkan Mae` bus stop on Tosaden Kotsu Bus or MY-YU Bus; about 30 minutes by car from Kochi IC
- **Suggested stay**: 1.5-2 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.ryoma-kinenkan.jp/country/en/

## メモ

- 既存の `Katsurahama Beach` と `Kochi Castle` は除外済みのため、近接する別施設でも単独で観光需要が立つものだけを残した。
- `Sunday Market` も有力候補だったが、既存の `Hirome Market` と食目的の役割が近いため、今回は県全体での知名度と独立性がより高い施設を優先した。
- `Chikurin-ji Temple` と `Makino Botanical Garden` は隣接するが、前者は高知の代表的寺院、後者は県を代表する植物園として観光上の役割が明確に異なるため両方を採用した。
