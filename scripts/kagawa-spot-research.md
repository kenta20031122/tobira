## 追加候補 5 件

### 1. Shikoku Aquarium / 四国水族館
- **Prefecture**: Kagawa
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: One of Kagawa's highest-profile modern attractions, this major aquarium showcases roughly 400 species connected to Shikoku's seas and rivers. Its sunset-facing dolphin pool and easy access from Utazu make it a mainstream stop for first-time visitors.
- **Address (English)**: 4 Hama 1-bancho, Utazu-cho, Ayauta-gun, Kagawa 769-0201, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.3128, 133.8081
- **Admission**: JPY 2,600 for adults
- **Best season**: Year-round; late afternoon is especially rewarding for the sunset deck
- **Access**: About 12 minutes on foot from JR Utazu Station, about 10 minutes by car from Sakaide IC, or about 40 minutes by car from Takamatsu Airport
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://shikoku-aquarium.jp/en/

### 2. NEW Reoma World / ニューレオマワールド
- **Prefecture**: Kagawa
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: One of Shikoku's largest theme parks, NEW Reoma World is a long-established leisure destination known for rides, seasonal flower displays, parades, and large-scale illuminations. It has stronger mainstream family appeal than smaller local attractions in inland Kagawa.
- **Address (English)**: 40-1 Kurikuma Nishi, Ayautacho, Marugame, Kagawa 761-2405, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.2120, 133.8784
- **Admission**: From JPY 2,000 for adults for park admission
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn; winter is also popular for illuminations
- **Access**: About 25 minutes by car from Sakaide IC or Zentsuji IC, about 15 minutes from Fuchu Lake Smart IC, or use the free shuttle / local bus from JR Sakaide Station, JR Utazu Station, JR Kotohira Station, or Kotoden Okada Station
- **Suggested stay**: 4-6 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.newreomaworld.com/en/

### 3. Sanuki Manno Park / 国営讃岐まんのう公園
- **Prefecture**: Kagawa
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: nature
- **Description**: Shikoku's only national government park is one of Kagawa's best-known flower and outdoor recreation destinations, with expansive grounds, cycling, playgrounds, and seasonal events. It is especially established as a repeat-visit spot for spring blooms, autumn kochia, and winter illumination.
- **Address (English)**: 4243-12 Yoshino, Manno-cho, Nakatado-gun, Kagawa 766-0023, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.1669, 133.8798
- **Admission**: JPY 450 for visitors age 15 and over
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn; winter is also popular during the illumination period
- **Access**: About 35 minutes by car from Sakaide IC, about 25 minutes from Zentsuji IC, about 15 minutes by taxi from JR Kotohira Station / Kotoden Kotohira Station / Kotoden Okada Station, or bus to `Manno-Koen-Guchi` plus a walk
- **Suggested stay**: 2.5-4 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://sanukimannopark.jp/en

### 4. Shikoku Mura Museum / 四国村ミウゼアム
- **Prefecture**: Kagawa
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: history
- **Description**: This open-air museum at the foot of Yashima gathers traditional buildings from across Shikoku, making it one of Takamatsu area's most established cultural sightseeing stops outside the better-known plateau itself. The grounds, relocated folk architecture, and Tadao Ando-designed gallery give it broad appeal beyond a niche local-history visit.
- **Address (English)**: 91 Yashima Nakamachi, Takamatsu, Kagawa 761-0112, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.3456, 134.1089
- **Admission**: JPY 1,600 for adults
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn, when walking the outdoor museum grounds is most comfortable
- **Access**: About 5 minutes on foot from Kotoden Yashima Station, about 10 minutes on foot from JR Yashima Station, or about 15 minutes by car from Takamatsu-chuo IC
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.shikokumura.or.jp/en/

### 5. Zenigata Sand Coin / 銭形砂絵
- **Prefecture**: Kagawa
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: history
- **Description**: Kannonji's giant Edo-period coin-shaped sand drawing is one of western Kagawa's signature landmarks and a classic luck-themed sightseeing stop. Its scale, long-running local upkeep, and regular night illumination make it one of the prefecture's most recognizable non-island photo spots.
- **Address (English)**: 14 Ariake-cho, Kanonji, Kagawa 768-0062, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.1341, 133.6423
- **Admission**: Free
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring and autumn are best for walking Kotohiki Park, and dusk is ideal for illumination views
- **Access**: About 12 minutes by car from Onohara IC, about 15 minutes from Sanuki Toyonaka IC, about 5 minutes by taxi from JR Kanonji Station, or local shared bus to `Kotohiki Park`
- **Suggested stay**: 45-90 minutes
- **Official site URL**: https://www.city.kanonji.kagawa.jp/soshiki/21/333.html

## メモ

- 5件とも香川県の主要観光案内で繰り返し紹介される定番級で、既存除外スポットを外した後でも知名度を維持できる候補を優先した。
- `Shikoku Aquarium` と `NEW Reoma World` は、県外旅行者にも通じる集客力の強い現代型施設として特に追加優先度が高い。
- `Sanuki Manno Park` は自然景観と体験需要を兼ねる大型定番スポットとして採用した。
- `Shikoku Mura Museum` は `Yashima Plateau` を除外したうえでも高松エリアで独立した目的地として成立する文化系の有力候補。
- `Zenigata Sand Coin` は観音寺市の象徴的名所で、既存の `Chichibugahama Beach` と同じ西讃エリアでも体験内容が重ならない。
