## 追加候補 5 件

### 1. Otsuka Museum of Art / 大塚国際美術館
- **Prefecture**: Tokushima
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: One of Tokushima's best-known indoor attractions, this major museum displays more than 1,000 full-size ceramic reproductions of Western masterpieces. Its scale, name recognition, and direct bus access make it a standard first-trip stop in Naruto beyond the whirlpools area.
- **Address (English)**: 65-1 Fukuike, Tosadomariura, Naruto-cho, Naruto, Tokushima 772-0053, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.236944, 134.633889
- **Admission**: JPY 3,300 for adults
- **Best season**: Year-round; especially useful in summer and rainy weather because it is primarily indoors
- **Access**: Direct highway buses stop at `Otsuka Kokusai Bijutsukan-mae`; from JR Naruto Station, take the Naruto City Bus toward Naruto Park and get off at `Naruto Kameura-guchi`, then walk a short distance; about 3 minutes by car from Naruto-kita IC
- **Suggested stay**: 2.5-4 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://o-museum.or.jp/en/

### 2. Oasahiko Shrine / 大麻比古神社
- **Prefecture**: Tokushima
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: spiritual
- **Description**: Widely regarded as Tokushima's leading shrine, Oasahiko Shrine is a classic visit known for its long lantern-lined approach, huge torii, and old-growth trees. It draws strong support both as a spiritual landmark and as one of the prefecture's best-known shrine visits.
- **Address (English)**: 13 Aza Hirozuka, Bando, Oasa-cho, Naruto, Tokushima 779-0230, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.170694, 134.502611
- **Admission**: Free
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring greenery and autumn foliage are especially appealing
- **Access**: About 25 minutes on foot from JR Bando Station, or about 10-15 minutes by car from Itano IC / Aizumi IC
- **Suggested stay**: 1-1.5 hours
- **Official site URL**: http://www.ooasahikojinja.jp/e/

### 3. Udatsu Townscape / うだつの町並み
- **Prefecture**: Tokushima
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: history
- **Description**: This preserved merchant district in Wakimachi is one of Tokushima's signature historic streets, lined with white-walled houses that reflect the wealth of the Awa indigo trade. It is one of the prefecture's most established walking destinations for visitors seeking an atmospheric old-town experience.
- **Address (English)**: Wakimachi Minamimachi, Mima, Tokushima 779-3610, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.068053, 134.146592
- **Admission**: Free
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn, when strolling the preserved townscape is most comfortable
- **Access**: About 10 minutes by car from Wakimachi IC, or take a Tokushima Seibu Kotsu bus from JR Anabuki Station to `Wakimachi Michi-no-eki` and walk about 10 minutes
- **Suggested stay**: 1.5-2.5 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://en.mimakankou.or.jp/event/udatsunomachinami/

### 4. Awa Jurobe Yashiki / 阿波十郎兵衛屋敷
- **Prefecture**: Tokushima
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: history
- **Description**: This former residence and puppet-theater complex is one of Tokushima's flagship places to experience Awa ningyo joruri, the prefecture's signature traditional performing art. Daily performances and strong cultural recognition make it a long-established sightseeing stop rather than a niche museum.
- **Address (English)**: 184 Miyajima Motoura, Kawauchi-cho, Tokushima, Tokushima 770-0114, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 34.091500, 134.584050
- **Admission**: JPY 410 for adults
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring and autumn are easiest for combining with wider Tokushima City sightseeing
- **Access**: From JR Tokushima Station, take the Kawauchi Loop / Tokushima City Bus from terminal B7 or B14 for about 20-25 minutes to `Awajurobe Yashiki-mae`; about 5 minutes by car from Tokushima IC
- **Suggested stay**: 1-1.5 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.joruri.info/jurobe/english/info.html

### 5. Tairyuji Temple / 太龍寺
- **Prefecture**: Tokushima
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: spiritual
- **Description**: Temple 21 of the Shikoku Pilgrimage is one of Tokushima's most famous mountain temples, known for its dramatic setting high above the forest and its reputation as `Western Koya`. The combination of pilgrimage importance and the long ropeway approach gives it stronger destination appeal than a routine temple stop.
- **Address (English)**: 2 Ryuzan, Kamo-cho, Anan, Tokushima 771-5173, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.882528, 134.521889
- **Admission**: Free for the temple grounds; Tairyuji Ropeway round trip is JPY 2,470 for adults
- **Best season**: Spring and autumn, especially for mountain scenery, fresh greenery, and fall foliage
- **Access**: Take the Tokushima Bus from JR Tokushima Station to `Wajikihigashi`, walk about 15 minutes to the Tairyuji Ropeway, then ride about 10 minutes to the upper station and walk to the temple; driving from Tokushima City takes roughly 50-60 minutes to the ropeway base
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.shikoku-cable.co.jp/tairyuji/

## メモ

- `Otsuka Museum of Art` は除外済みの `Naruto Whirlpools` と同じ鳴門エリアだが、完全に別目的で集客できる県内トップ級の定番施設として採用。
- `Oasahiko Shrine` は初詣需要でも知られる徳島の代表格の神社で、知名度・安定性の両面で追加候補として強い。
- `Udatsu Townscape` と `Awa Jurobe Yashiki` はどちらも文化系だが、前者は町並み散策、後者は郷土芸能鑑賞で体験内容が明確に異なる。
- `Tairyuji Temple` 自体の独立公式サイトは確認できず、来訪実務では公式のロープウェイ案内が最も有用なため、それを `Official site URL` に記載した。
