| 大三島 | access | Ferry from Imabari, 35 min | Passenger boat about 35 min; or car ferry about 55 min. Or cycle Shimanami Kaido |
| 大洲城 | access | 15 min walk from Iyo-Ozu station | About 27 min walk from JR Iyo-Ozu Station, or about 15 min by bicycle (rental at station) |
| 四国カルスト | access | 50 min drive from Matsuyama | About 1 h 45 min–2 h drive from Matsuyama (approx. 75 km or 85 km); no direct public transport |
| しまなみ海道（愛媛側） | admission | Bike rental ¥3,000; bridge tolls ¥500 | Bike rental from about ¥2,000/day (electric about ¥2,500). Bridge tolls: free until 31 Mar 2026; otherwise ¥500 total |
