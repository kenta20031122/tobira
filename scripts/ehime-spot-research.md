選定方針:
- 既存候補で相対的に弱い `food` `activity` `onsen` を優先
- 県内で定番として定着しており、通常営業している有名スポットに限定
- 既存の城・寺・町並み・絶景岬・サイクリングと役割が重なりすぎる候補は外す

## 追加候補 5 件

### 1. Tobe Zoological Park / 愛媛県立とべ動物園
- **Prefecture**: Ehime
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: Western Japan's largest zoo is one of Ehime's best-known family attractions, with polar bears, elephants, and immersive habitat-style exhibits. It works well as a half-day outing from Matsuyama and is established as a year-round mainstream stop.
- **Address (English)**: 240 Kamiharamachi, Tobe-cho, Iyo-gun, Ehime 791-2191, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.764301, 132.791942
- **Admission**: JPY 600 for adults
- **Best season**: Year-round; spring and autumn are the most comfortable for walking
- **Access**: About 10 minutes by car from Matsuyama IC, or about 36 minutes by Iyotetsu bus from Matsuyama City Station to `Tobe Dobutsuen-mae` plus a short walk
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.tobezoo.com/en/

### 2. Minetopia Besshi / マイントピア別子
- **Prefecture**: Ehime
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: activity
- **Description**: This major industrial-heritage park lets visitors ride a mine train, enter a sightseeing tunnel, and experience the story of the Besshi Copper Mine that supported Japan's modernization. It is one of eastern Ehime's signature hands-on attractions and operates as a well-established sightseeing complex.
- **Address (English)**: 707-3 Tatsukawacho, Niihama, Ehime 792-0846, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.901527, 133.309483
- **Admission**: Free entry to the roadside-station area; mine sightseeing is paid and starts from about JPY 1,300
- **Best season**: Spring to autumn; the outdoor industrial-heritage scenery is especially pleasant in fresh greenery and autumn foliage
- **Access**: About 15 minutes by car from the Niihama IC area, or take the Setouchi Bus `Yamane / Minetopia Line` toward `Minetopia Besshi`
- **Suggested stay**: 2-3 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://besshi.com/

### 3. Michi-no-Eki Minato Oasis Yawatahama Minatto / 道の駅 みなとオアシス 八幡浜みなっと
- **Prefecture**: Ehime
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: food
- **Description**: Yawatahama's best-known food stop combines a seafood market, local-produce shops, and dining focused on fresh port-town flavors. It is a reliable, established stop for seafood, citrus products, and casual harbor-side strolling.
- **Address (English)**: 1581-23 Okishinden, Yawatahama, Ehime 796-0087, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.458230, 132.418314
- **Admission**: Free
- **Best season**: Year-round; autumn to winter is especially good for local citrus and cold-season seafood
- **Access**: About 5 minutes by car from JR Yawatahama Station, about 25 minutes from Ozu IC, or about 3 minutes on foot from Yawatahama Ferry Terminal
- **Suggested stay**: 1-2 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.minatto.net/english

### 4. Michi-no-Eki Minato Oasis Uwajima Kisaiya Hiroba / 道の駅 みなとオアシスうわじま きさいや広場
- **Prefecture**: Ehime
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: food
- **Description**: This famous Uwajima roadside station is one of the easiest places to sample local specialties such as Uwajima taimeshi and jakoten in one stop. It also functions as a popular souvenir and produce hub near Uwajima Port, making it a mainstream culinary stop in southern Ehime.
- **Address (English)**: 1-318-16 Bentencho, Uwajima, Ehime 798-0006, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.222322, 132.558414
- **Admission**: Free
- **Best season**: Year-round; especially good in cooler months for seafood-focused meals and local produce shopping
- **Access**: Right off the Uwajima-Asahi IC area by car, or about 12 minutes on foot from JR Uwajima Station
- **Suggested stay**: 1-2 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://www.kisaiyahiroba.com/

### 5. Nibukawa Seseragi Koryukan / 鈍川せせらぎ交流館
- **Prefecture**: Ehime
- **Region**: shikoku
- **Main category**: onsen
- **Description**: A popular day-use bath at the entrance to the historic Nibukawa Onsen area, this facility is known for alkaline waters often described as `beauty bath` water. It is one of Ehime's most established non-Dogo onsen stops and is surrounded by quiet valley scenery.
- **Address (English)**: 218-1 Nibukawako, Tamagawa-cho, Imabari, Ehime 794-0121, Japan
- **Coordinates**: 33.984671, 132.940372
- **Admission**: JPY 560 for adults
- **Best season**: Autumn and winter; the valley setting is strongest in foliage season and cool-weather bathing
- **Access**: About 30 minutes by bus from JR Imabari Station on a bus via Nibukawa Onsen to `Seseragi Koryukan`, or about 20 minutes by car from Imabari IC
- **Suggested stay**: 1.5-2 hours
- **Official site URL**: https://seseragi-onsen.com/

## メモ

- 5件の内訳は `activity` 2件、`food` 2件、`onsen` 1件。
- 既存の `Dogo Onsen` 以外の温泉補強としては `Nibukawa Seseragi Koryukan` が最も扱いやすい。
- `food` は個店ではなく、観光地として確立した道の駅・港湾拠点を選び、通常営業の安定性を優先した。
- `Minetopia Besshi` は歴史要素も強いが、今回の不足カテゴリ補完を優先して `activity` に寄せて整理した。
